About

My Chocolate World is born out of the chocolate cravings of choco lovers, featuring high-end luxury chocolate brands from all over the world.

What sets apart My Chocolate World is it evokes memories, nostalgia, emotions and a bunch of other feelings to come together by consuming the favorite chocolates from around the world. Back in olden times, we all happened to have a good deal going with our family traveling abroad. Nobody ever comes back without a bunch of confections from our favorite international chocolate brands. Cut to present

My Chocolate World shelves a collection of more than 100 varieties of fine imported and artisanal handmade chocolates for your easy access, whenever you crave for. Encased in aesthetically opulent finery, our craft goes beyond chocolates to celebrate sweetness with a dedicated gifting lounge; that features an exquisite chocolate collection from boutique chocolate brands to customize a hamper for every occasion.

With an extravagant chocolate collection from famous international brands, we aim to sate the cravings of all chocolate connoisseurs with goodies that lift your spirits.
